# One-shot Examples for Expert Feedback

Please write one feedback response for each A1 learner essay below.

Feedback requirements:
- Maximum 70 words per feedback response.
- Focus only on structure and organisation.
- Do not comment on grammar, spelling, or vocabulary.
- Do not rewrite the essay.
- Use encouraging, simple language suitable for an A1 learner.
- Give one clear suggestion for improving the structure.

## Narrative Example

Writing ID: 696

Task topic: Updating your online profile

Learner essay:

> Hi! My name's Ronaldo. I'm twenty-nine years old. I'm from Brazil. I live at Ceara State, in northeastern brazilian coast. I'm a fisheries engineer and I have been a graduate student as member of the Animal Ecology Laboratory, at Federal University of Ceara. Best wishes, Ronaldo

Expert feedback:

> Your profile is easy to follow because you tell the reader about yourself in a clear order. First your personal details, then your work and study. This is good. One suggestion is to group the information about your job and university together at the end to make it look more organized.

## Descriptive Example

Writing ID: 130

Task topic: Making a dinner party menu

Learner essay:

> Hi! This is the menu that I will do to dinner, see if you like: Starter: Vegetables and soup. Drinks: Water, beer, wine and whisk. Main course: Roast beef, rice, noodles and potato chip. Dessert: Ice Cream and maracuj cream.

Expert feedback:

> Your menu is easy to read because you put the food into different parts like starter, drinks, main course, and dessert. This helps the reader understand your plan. One suggestion is to write one short sentence at the beginning to tell the reader this is your dinner party menu.
